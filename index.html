ABOUT US

Perfection with Quickness & Commitment

Tusker Group is a leading Security and Facility Management Company with its corporate office in Gurgaon. As a group our focus lies in designing services which makes a visibly difference for our clients and to their businesses. We tailor our services to look at the smallest details that can create a great customer experience. We are supported by our talented team and backed up by leading-edge technology along with huge experience in providing services in Facility & Property Management, Security Services, Detective & Investigation Services, Electronic Security, Manpower outsourcing and other related services. Tusker has a unique approach that builds long lasting relationships with our customers based on our expertise and values.
